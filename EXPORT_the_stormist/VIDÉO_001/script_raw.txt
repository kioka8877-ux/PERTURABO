Tropical storm. A tropical storm has sustained winds between 39 and 73 miles per hour...

[DÉVELOPPEMENT]
Category 1 hurricane. A Category 1 hurricane has sustained winds between 74 and 95 miles per hour...
Category 3 hurricane. Category 3 is the threshold for what meteorologists classify as a major hurricane...
Category 5 hurricane. Category 5 is the highest classification on the Saffir-Simpson scale...
Super typhoon. A super typhoon is a typhoon with sustained winds of 150 miles per hour or greater...
Cyclone. In the Indian Ocean and South Pacific, the same type of storm is called a cyclone...
Bomb cyclone. A bomb cyclone occurs when a storm's central pressure drops by at least 24 millibars in 24 hours...

[CHUTE/CTA]
Hypercane. A hypercane is a hypothetical tropical cyclone that has never been observed...
